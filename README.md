🚦 Road Accident Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

📊 Project Overview

The Road Accident Analysis Dashboard is an interactive data visualization project built using Power BI to analyze road accident data across the United Kingdom for the years 2021 and 2022.
The objective of this project is to uncover insights into accident trends, casualty severity, vehicle involvement, road conditions, and geographic distribution. The dashboard enables stakeholders to identify high-risk locations and contributing factors, helping support data-driven road safety decisions.
This project demonstrates skills in data cleaning, SQL data validation, data modeling, DAX calculations, and dashboard storytelling.

🎯 Project Objectives

The key objectives of this dashboard are to:

1. Analyze total accidents and casualties for the current year.
2. Compare Current Year vs Previous Year performance (YoY growth).
3. Understand accident distribution by severity level.
4. Identify the impact of vehicle type, road type, and environmental conditions on accidents.
5. Detect accident hotspots using geospatial visualization.
6. Provide insights that can support improvements in road safety strategies.

📈 Dashboard Features

1️⃣ Casualties by Vehicle Type

Displays the number of casualties caused by different vehicle categories.
Key Observation:
Cars contribute the highest number of casualties (155K+), followed by vans and bikes.

2️⃣ Monthly Casualties Trend (CY vs PY)

A line chart comparison between Current Year and Previous Year casualties.
Insights:
Casualties gradually increase throughout the year
Peak accident months occur around October and November
Overall decline in casualties in 2022 compared to 2021

3️⃣ Casualties by Urban vs Rural Areas

Breakdown of accidents by area type:
Urban: ~62%
Rural: ~38%
📌 Insight:
Higher accidents occur in urban regions due to dense traffic, population concentration, and complex road interactions.

4️⃣ Casualties by Road Type

Road Type	Casualties
Single Carriageway	145K
Dual Carriageway	32K
Roundabout	13K
One Way Street	3K
Slip Road	2K
📌 Insight:
Single carriageway roads have the highest accident rate, likely due to two-way traffic sharing the same road space.

5️⃣ Casualties by Light Conditions

Breakdown of accidents based on lighting conditions:
Daytime: 73.84%
Night/Dark: 26.16%
📌 Insight:
Most accidents occur during daytime hours, primarily due to higher traffic volume and road activity.

6️⃣ Geographic Accident Distribution

A map visualization highlights accident locations across the United Kingdom, enabling users to identify high-risk accident clusters and regional accident patterns.

🔍 Key Insights

1. Some of the most important insights derived from the dashboard:
2. Total casualties decreased by 11.9% year-over-year
3. Cars account for the majority of casualties
4. Urban areas experience significantly more accidents than rural areas
5. Single carriageway roads show the highest accident frequency
6. Accident rates increase toward the end of the year

These insights can help authorities prioritize road safety interventions, improve infrastructure planning, and implement targeted safety policies.
