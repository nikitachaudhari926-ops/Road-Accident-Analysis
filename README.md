🚦 Road Accident Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

📊 Project Overview

The Road Accident Analysis Dashboard is an interactive data visualization project built using Power BI to analyze road accident data across the United Kingdom for the years 2021 and 2022.
The objective of this project is to provide insights into accident trends, casualty severity, vehicle involvement, road conditions, and geographical distribution of accidents. The dashboard enables stakeholders to identify high-risk areas and factors contributing to accidents, helping support data-driven road safety decisions. This project demonstrates skills in data cleaning, data modeling, DAX calculations, and dashboard storytelling.


🎯 Project Objectives

The main objectives of this dashboard are to:
1. Analyze total accidents and casualties for the current year.
2. Compare Current Year vs Previous Year performance (YoY growth).
3. Understand accident distribution by severity level.
4. Identify the impact of vehicle type, road type, and environmental conditions on accidents.
5. Detect accident hotspots using geospatial visualization.
6. Provide insights that could help improve road safety strategies.

📈 Dashboard Features :- 
The dashboard contains several analytical components to explore accident patterns:

1️⃣ Casualties by Vehicle Type :- 

Displays the number of casualties caused by different vehicle types.
Key observation :- 
Cars contribute the highest number of casualties (155K+)
Followed by Vans and Bikes

2️⃣ Monthly Casualties Trend (CY vs PY)

A line chart comparing monthly casualties between Current Year and Previous Year.
Insights:
Casualties gradually increase throughout the year
Peak accident months occur around October and November
Overall decline in casualties in 2022 compared to 2021

3️⃣ Casualties by Urban vs Rural Areas

Breakdown of casualties by area type:
Urban: ~62%
Rural: ~38%
📌 Higher accidents occur in urban regions due to dense traffic and population concentration.

4️⃣ Casualties by Road Type

Identifies accident distribution across different road structures.
Road Type	Casualties
Single Carriageway	145K
Dual Carriageway	32K
Roundabout	13K
One Way Street	3K
Slip Road	2K
📌 Single carriageway roads have the highest accident rate, possibly due to two-way traffic sharing the same road.

5️⃣ Casualties by Light Conditions

Breakdown of accidents during different lighting conditions:
Daytime: 73.84%
Night/Dark: 26.16%
📌 Most accidents occur during the day due to higher traffic activity.

6️⃣ Geographic Accident Distribution

A map visualization highlights accident locations across the United Kingdom, helping identify high-risk accident clusters. This feature enables location-based accident analysis.

🔍 Key Insights

Some of the most important insights derived from the dashboard:
Total casualties decreased by 11.9% year-over-year
Cars are responsible for the majority of casualties
Urban areas experience significantly more accidents than rural areas
Single carriageway roads show the highest accident frequency
Accident rates increase toward the end of the year
These insights can help authorities prioritize road safety interventions and infrastructure improvements.
